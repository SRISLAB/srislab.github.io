Our Team

The SRIS Lab brings together a diverse team of researchers passionate about building Smart, Reliable, and Interpretable Systems. We are united by our commitment to advancing the frontier of trustworthy AI for intelligent manufacturing and critical infrastructure.

Our team includes faculty members, postdoctoral researchers, PhD and Master's students, and undergraduate researchers—all working collaboratively to tackle fundamental challenges at the intersection of logic, physics, and machine learning.

Principal Investigator

陈 刚 · Gang Chen

Associate Professor, South China University of Technology
gangchen@scut.edu.cn
Guangzhou International Campus

Dr. Gang Chen received his Ph.D. from UC Davis and was a postdoctoral researcher at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. His research interests span machine learning, formal methods, control theory, and signal processing, with a focus on interpretable fault diagnosis and trustworthy cyber-physical systems. He leads the SRIS Lab in developing unified methodologies that combine Signal Temporal Logic (STL), causal inference, and physics-guided priors for building smart, reliable, and interpretable systems.
